## Why today's pick
Two critical security items are already in-progress on the same branch (feat/auth-and-rls) and are tightly coupled — JWT verification is meaningless without RLS, and RLS is meaningless without JWT. Shipping them together is the honest move. The runner-up was b_cc01 (missing migrations on the same branch), but it's a schema/repro concern rather than an active exploit surface, so it goes to upNext where it can be folded in once auth lands. Avoided nominating any of the workaround-in-place or low-priority items — none of them deserve attention while the security spine is unfinished.
