# FlowOS · User Personas & User Stories

> 4 personas · mix of role and business type · end-to-end interaction with Flow
> Format: As a [persona], I want to [action] so that [outcome].

---

## Persona 1 · Solo DTC Founder
### *e.g. MVEDA — Ayurvedic skincare, runs marketing solo*

---

### Setup

**US-1.1**
As a solo DTC founder, I want to paste my brand URL and have FlowOS scrape my site so that I get an instant brand profile — voice, palette, values, competitor signals — without spending hours filling in forms.

**US-1.2**
As a solo DTC founder, I want to review and confirm extracted prohibited phrases in Brand Memory so that FlowOS never publishes anything that contradicts my Ayurvedic philosophy or uses clinical claims I can't back up.

**US-1.3**
As a solo DTC founder, I want FlowOS to recommend which connectors to activate based on my brand type so that I'm not browsing 20+ integrations figuring out where to start.

---

### Daily Creation

**US-1.4**
As a solo DTC founder, I want to type "write me an Instagram carousel about neem for oily skin" in the chat rail so that Drafter returns a ready-to-review card I can push to queue in one click — without opening another tool.

**US-1.5**
As a solo DTC founder, I want proactive drafts to appear in my Drafts strip each morning based on upcoming calendar moments and trending ingredients so that I always have content ready even when I'm too busy to think of ideas.

**US-1.6**
As a solo DTC founder, I want to generate a product image via Runware from within the draft card — using my brand palette and a natural prompt — so that I don't need a photographer for every post.

---

### Publishing & Scheduling

**US-1.7**
As a solo DTC founder, I want to see my full week in the calendar grid so that I can spot gaps and feel confident I'm showing up consistently across channels.

**US-1.8**
As a solo DTC founder, I want to set Instagram and email to auto-publish approved content so that posts go out on schedule even when I'm fulfilling orders, with Brand Guard checking each piece silently before it goes live.

---

### Inbox & CX

**US-1.9**
As a solo DTC founder, I want the Inbox to sort my DMs and comments into "Needs a decision" and "Ready to send" so that I handle sensitive questions myself while routine replies go out without eating my day.

---

### Analytics & Optimisation

**US-1.10**
As a solo DTC founder, I want to ask the Analyst "which posts drove the most link clicks last month?" in plain language so that I understand what's converting — without building a spreadsheet.

---

### Governance & Scale

**US-1.11**
As a solo DTC founder, I want A/B test winners to automatically update Brand Memory with the winning tone so that my content gets sharper over time without me tracking it manually.

**US-1.12**
As a solo DTC founder, I want to invite a part-time contractor as a guest with view-only access to the queue so that they can help stage content without touching Brand Memory or autonomy settings.

## Persona 2 · Marketing Manager, B2B Service Company
### *Regional IT managed services firm, 2-person marketing team*

---

### Setup

**US-2.1**
As a marketing manager at a B2B service company, I want to import our website so that FlowOS extracts our positioning, service language, and approved claims — and pre-populates Brand Memory with compliance-safe guardrails from day one.

**US-2.2**
As a marketing manager at a B2B service company, I want to connect GA4, LinkedIn Pages, and our email platform in one Connections flow so that all performance data surfaces in a single place.

---

### Daily Creation

**US-2.3**
As a marketing manager at a B2B service company, I want to brief the Drafter with "write a LinkedIn post announcing our SOC 2 certification — formal tone, no hype" so that the output is already within our voice guidelines and I spend minutes editing rather than hours drafting.

**US-2.4**
As a marketing manager at a B2B service company, I want Brand Guard to flag any draft using unverified security claims before it reaches the queue so that we never publish something that creates a compliance or reputational risk.

---

### Publishing & Scheduling

**US-2.5**
As a marketing manager at a B2B service company, I want to schedule a 4-week LinkedIn and email campaign from a single calendar view so that I can see content frequency and ensure we're not going silent during key sales cycles.

**US-2.6**
As a marketing manager at a B2B service company, I want my team member's drafts to land in a "pending review" lane rather than auto-publish so that I retain final approval on all external communications.

---

### Inbox & CX

**US-2.7**
As a marketing manager at a B2B service company, I want the Inbox agent to surface email replies from nurture sequences that signal buying intent — separated from out-of-office responses — so that I can flag hot leads to sales immediately.

---

### Analytics & Optimisation

**US-2.8**
As a marketing manager at a B2B service company, I want to ask "how did our last three LinkedIn articles perform vs short-form posts?" so that I can make a data-backed case for where to invest content effort next quarter.

**US-2.9**
As a marketing manager at a B2B service company, I want the channel strategy agent to recommend budget allocation across LinkedIn Ads, Google Ads, and email based on our pipeline goals so that I can present a justified media plan without commissioning an external consultant.

---

### Governance & Scale

**US-2.10**
As a marketing manager at a B2B service company, I want to run subject-line A/B tests on email sequences and have winners applied automatically to future sends so that email performance improves continuously without manual iteration.

**US-2.11**
As a marketing manager at a B2B service company, I want to create distinct tone modes in Brand Memory — one for thought leadership, one for service announcements — so that Drafter applies the right register automatically by content type.

## Persona 3 · Agency Account Manager
### *Boutique social media agency, manages 5 client accounts*

---

### Setup

**US-3.1**
As an agency account manager, I want to create a separate workspace per client and import their brand URL so that voice, palette, Brand Memory, and publishing queues are fully isolated — no cross-contamination between accounts.

**US-3.2**
As an agency account manager, I want to invite each client as a guest with access scoped to their own queue and Inbox only so that they can review and approve content without seeing other clients' data or our internal workflows.

---

### Daily Creation

**US-3.3**
As an agency account manager, I want to brief the Drafter per client workspace — "write a week of Instagram captions for a sustainable pet food brand, playful tone" — so that the AI produces on-brand drafts per account without me rewriting from scratch each time.

**US-3.4**
As an agency account manager, I want Brand Guard to check every draft against that specific client's Brand Memory — banned phrases, approved claims — so that nothing ships that contradicts what we agreed in onboarding.

**US-3.5**
As an agency account manager, I want to generate UGC-style product videos using HeyGen from within the draft card so that I can offer clients video creative without a production budget or freelance videographer.

---

### Publishing & Scheduling

**US-3.6**
As an agency account manager, I want to toggle between client workspaces in the calendar view so that I can see all five publishing schedules in a single session and identify where I'm over- or under-servicing accounts.

**US-3.7**
As an agency account manager, I want to set each client's autonomy rules independently — some auto-publish, some require client approval — so that I can match our service agreement per account without building a separate process for each.

---

### Inbox & CX

**US-3.8**
As an agency account manager, I want the Inbox to triage each client's DMs and comments into urgency tiers and show me the AI's reasoning before surfacing a suggested reply so that I can validate tone and accuracy before anything sends — especially for clients I know are sensitive.

---

### Analytics & Optimisation

**US-3.9**
As an agency account manager, I want to pull a per-client performance summary — reach, engagement, click-throughs — from the Analyst agent so that I can prepare monthly reporting decks without manually exporting from Meta Business Suite and GA4 separately.

---

### Governance & Scale

**US-3.10**
As an agency account manager, I want A/B results per client to update that client's Brand Memory with the winning creative direction so that each account improves autonomously and I can show clients measurable progress quarter over quarter.

**US-3.11**
As an agency account manager, I want to assign specific chat rail workstreams to team members per client workspace so that my creative team works inside the right brand context without account-crossing errors or mixed-up briefs.

## Persona 4 · Head of Growth, Mid-Size E-Commerce Brand
### *$20M+ DTC, 12-person growth team, high volume, performance-focused*

---

### Setup

**US-4.1**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want to connect Meta Ads, Google Ads, TikTok Ads, Klaviyo, GA4, Ahrefs, and Shopify in a single Connections setup so that FlowOS has a complete picture of our stack from day one and the Analyst has full data access.

**US-4.2**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want extracted competitor signals to automatically inform the Analyst's benchmarking baseline so that our performance is always contextualised against our actual competitive landscape.

---

### Daily Creation

**US-4.3**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want the system to auto-generate proactive draft batches tied to our promotional calendar and product launches so that the content team is never starting from a blank page and throughput stays high.

**US-4.4**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want to generate multiple creative variants per post — different headlines, hooks, Runware images — so that we can run structured A/B tests across Meta and TikTok without a separate creative ops workflow.

**US-4.5**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want to produce cinematic product videos using Runway from within a draft card so that paid ad creative can be produced in-house at scale without a video production retainer.

---

### Publishing & Scheduling

**US-4.6**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want to manage our full multi-channel queue — Meta, TikTok, email, SMS, LinkedIn — from a single calendar grid so that campaign timing is coordinated and nothing conflicts across channels.

**US-4.7**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want to configure autonomy per channel — auto-publish on organic social, require my sign-off on paid ad copy — so that routine content ships without bottlenecks while high-stakes placements always get human review.

---

### Inbox & CX

**US-4.8**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want the Inbox to triage high-volume post-purchase email replies and DMs — separating refund escalations from general questions — so that my CX team focuses on revenue-at-risk conversations first.

---

### Analytics & Optimisation

**US-4.9**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want to ask "what's our blended CAC across Meta, TikTok, and Google this month vs last quarter?" so that I get a cross-channel performance read instantly — without reconciling three ad platforms in a spreadsheet.

**US-4.10**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want the channel strategy agent to recommend budget reallocation based on current ROAS trends and our monthly revenue target so that I make reallocation decisions with AI-assisted justification rather than gut feel.

---

### Governance & Scale

**US-4.11**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want A/B winners on ad copy and creative to be auto-promoted to Brand Memory so that high-performing hooks are institutionalised and every new draft starts from a stronger baseline.

**US-4.12**
As a head of growth at a mid-size e-commerce brand, I want role-scoped team access — copywriters see Drafter and queue, media buyers see Analyst and paid connectors, brand lead controls Brand Memory — so that everyone works in their lane without accidentally overriding brand rules or publishing off-strategy.
